Loading...

Interactive Narrative HTML Story

Loading... is an interactive story where your choices determine your ending. Without spoiling too much, it's set in the not-so-distant future where you must uncover what this computer holds about the past and for the future. I wrote this piece since I was inspired by Adam Cadre and his Interactive Fiction piece, Varicella. If you haven't played it, I highly recommend you do. From it I draw inspiration for the voice, speed, and focalization of my own piece. Enjoy!
