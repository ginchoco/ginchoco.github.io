Memory Believing Before Knowing Remembers

A sonnet written around the same time.

        It is of chaos and confusion here
        Ne’er sun so loud, or sphere so farce as she
        To whom who watches be the quiet seer
        As warmth ensues; the heart will course to beat
        Find pulled lightness from faint and unclear mind
        Brought forth elated calm in nature’s rest
        How to compare the chirp and buzz entwined,
        Or slight breath of a summer's wind to test
        Where is creation once this place hath set
        It was hidden, do you see it so far?
        The uncover’d world from above calls a bet
        To try to fall into the blue noir
        And the tinge of sorrow from being a part
